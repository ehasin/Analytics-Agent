How the architecture earns that trust

Before SQLCheck the question against a business-curated semantic model: valid use cases, metrics, field context, and known limits.
During SQLFormulate safe queries against governed metadata and return structured evidence.
After SQLValidate the narrative against the evidence: numbers, claims, caveats, and uncertainty.
